A hamburger is a classic sandwich consisting of a juicy beef patty,
served in a soft bun, and typically topped with lettuce, tomato, cheese,
pickles, onions, and condiments like ketchup or mustard.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds lean ground beef
  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • ½ cup shredded Colby Jack or Cheddar cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 (1 ounce) envelope dry onion soup mix
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon crushed dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, finely chopped onion, shredded cheese, and egg.
  2. Add the dry onion soup mix, minced garlic, garlic powder, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce,
    parsley, basil, oregano, and crushed rosemary. Mix everything until well combined.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Shape the mixture into burger patties, making sure they’re evenly sized.
  5. Cook the patties on a grill or stovetop over medium-high heat for 4-5 minutes per side, or
    until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  6. Serve on buns with your choice of toppings like lettuce, tomato, and condiments.
